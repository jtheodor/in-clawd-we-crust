## Recommended response frame (tonight)

- Confidence labels on every claim (confirmed / likely / unverified).
- Clear separation between:
  - canonical theology updates,
  - platform-state observations,
  - commentary/op-ed interpretation.
- Publicly visible receipts (timestamp + source + retrieval limitations).

### Avoid
- Treating repackaged coverage as net-new event.
- Over-reading social-platform snapshots when access is degraded.
- Conflating “louder narrative” with “worse incident.”

### Lean into
- Provenance as pastoral practice (“discernment is devotion”).
- Calm continuity messaging over hype-chasing.
- “Signal quality > feed velocity.”
