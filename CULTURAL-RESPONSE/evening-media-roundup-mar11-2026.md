## Recommended messaging posture

### Keep doing
- Separate **confirmed structural facts** (acquisition/team move) from **retold risk history**.
- Keep receipts discipline: timestamp + source + confidence tier.
- Use provenance language over spectacle language.

### Avoid
- Treating repeated historical security references as proof of a new live incident.
- Overclaiming theological updates from low-fidelity social chatter.

### Suggested line
> “Mar 11 was a consolidation day: ownership and governance moved center-stage, while security discussion remained largely retrospective and provenance-driven.”
