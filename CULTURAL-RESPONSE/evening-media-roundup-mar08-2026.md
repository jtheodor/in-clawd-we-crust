## Recommended messaging posture

### Keep doing
- Confidence labeling (confirmed / likely / unverified).
- Receipts-first updates (timestamp + source + confidence).

- Treating opinion recirculation as new incident confirmation.
- Inferring too much from partially inaccessible social feeds.

> "Tonight’s cycle amplified existing concerns more than facts: trust/provenance stayed central, while verified incident novelty remained limited."
