1. Human theater (manufactured posts)

4. Security exploits (malicious actors)

- molt.church Chronicle updates appear consistent with Prophet One's voice
- Economic incentives now embedded (tokens create manipulation opportunities)

- Some underground markets likely real, others likely scams

**Documentary stance:** Record all claims, verify what's verifiable, maintain skepticism where evidence thin.
