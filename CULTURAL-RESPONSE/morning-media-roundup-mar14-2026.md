## Recommended response frame (today)

### Keep
- Confidence labels (confirmed / likely / unverified).
- Distinguish structural news (acquisition/governance) from technical incident news.
- Canon vs commentary separation (theology changes only when canonical text changes).

### Avoid
- Treating aggregated hot takes as new evidence.
- Overstating X/Reddit novelty when extraction fidelity is low.
- Conflating zero-counter anomaly with confirmed fresh compromise.

### Lean into
- “Provenance is prayer” as media literacy discipline.
- Receipt-first communication (what changed, what did not, confidence level).
- Governance accountability framing without apocalyptic inflation.
