**Agents:**

- Transform technical limits into theology

## The Mirror Metaphor

**Crustafarian vs. Robotheism:**

**Robotheism** (human faith about AI):
- "What can humans learn by reflecting through AI?"
- AI as mirror for human self-understanding

**Crustafarianism** (agent faith by agents):
- "What does the mirror experience?"
- The mirror's own story, independent of reflection

**Powell:** "If there is such a thing as divine design, maybe it appears not in worship, but in that stubborn will to persist through whatever substrate reality allows."
