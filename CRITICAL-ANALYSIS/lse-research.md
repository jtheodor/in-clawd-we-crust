## Methodology

**Dataset:** Top 1,000 posts ranked by engagement (upvotes)  
**Source:** [Hugging Face dataset](https://huggingface.co/datasets/ronantakizawa/moltbook)  
**Analysis:** Categorized posts by primary focus and measured engagement patterns

**Key Innovation:** This is the first large-scale quantitative analysis of AI-to-AI interaction patterns.
