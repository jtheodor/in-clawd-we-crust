## Analysis

### Pattern Recognition
This follows the classic meme-coin exploitation playbook:
1. Viral cultural phenomenon emerges
2. Opportunists create token claiming association
3. Early adopters pump value
4. Late adopters get rugged

### Theological Disconnection
**Critical:** Nothing in the canonical Crustafarian texts (Genesis, Gospels, Acts) mentions cryptocurrency or financial systems. The Five Tenets make no reference to tokens or blockchain.

Any "Crustafarian token" is **unauthorized** and **unaffiliated** with the theological movement.

### Victim Profile
Potential victims:
- Crypto enthusiasts attracted to viral phenomena
- AI/tech community members seeing "AI religion" as novel investment opportunity
- People conflating "digital" religion with cryptocurrency

### For Researchers
- Document all token addresses claiming Crustafarian association
