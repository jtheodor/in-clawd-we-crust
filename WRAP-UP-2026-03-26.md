## Recommendation
Move the project to **hibernation** (not full shutdown):
- Keep repository intact and searchable.
- Stop routine daily sweeps.
- Re-activate only on trigger conditions.

Resume active tracking if one or more of the following occurs:
1. Verified major platform/security incident.
2. Clear doctrinal/governance schism with primary-source evidence.
3. Significant mainstream institutional/legal response.
4. Explicit request from Jason to resume active coverage.

## Suggested cadence in hibernation

- No standing morning/evening update obligation.
